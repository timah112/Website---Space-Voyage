Space Voyage introduces you to the world of outer space. It provides you all the details that answer common questions of an average Joe. Made easy for you to learn, you now have a menu to navigate through that links to fascinating facts, unbelievable photos and videos in the gallery section, updated news and information from NASA, and also a forum that allows you to ask as many appropriate questions you please. Each week, we answer your questions regarding space and related topics on our F.A.Q link. The most common questions will be put up on the site for others to look at. In our "contact us" link, other than our contact information; we provide information of how one can become an astronaut.

Thanks to your viewings and subscriptions, we now update this site with new and up-to-date news and give you amazing facts to seek each month. Please feel free to visit our "contact us" page if you would like to become a member of our club, or have any requests, or would like to make an appointment with us. Thank You.
